What does it do?

This platform provides a social network for buildings and enables easy communication and accessibility to information between Building Managers and Tenants and also allows Building Managers to better manage their properties while creating valuable neighbourhood connections.

How do I create a board?

Choose the board type, enter board name, add description, choose board visibility & access days, map out boundary area.

How do I populate the board with my people?

Click on board to invite people, click on ''invite members'', type in e-mails of persons & click on ''send invitation''. Board will be populated as people are join the board.

How do I create a townhall meeting?

Click on the ''townhall tab'', Click on ''create townhall meeting'', enter the title & a brief description, meeting start, end date & time and finally click on ''create town hall meeting.''
